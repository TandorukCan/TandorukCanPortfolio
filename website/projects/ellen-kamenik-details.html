Portfolio Details

For a client looking to transition their website from Wix, I developed a single-page application that replicates the exact design and functionality of their current site. Using React.js, I ensured that the new version maintains the same aesthetic and user experience while offering the benefits of modern web development practices. I also implemented Formik for form validation, ensuring that the contact form is both reliable and user-friendly.
